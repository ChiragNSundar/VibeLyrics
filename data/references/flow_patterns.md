# Hip-Hop Flow Patterns & Rhyme Schemes

## AABB (Couplet) Pattern
Lines that rhyme in pairs - classic hip-hop structure

"I came to win, that's the mission I'm in"
"Stack my dollars, never foldin' or thin"
"Every move calculated, precise with the timing"
"While my competition fading, I keep climbing"

## ABAB (Alternate) Pattern
Alternating rhymes for complex storytelling

"Started in the struggle, grinding for the pay"
"Nothing came easy, had to find my own way"
"Doubters kept talking, but I blocked out the noise"
"Now I'm living proof, turned a dream into joy"

## Internal Rhyme Examples
Rhymes within the same line

"I stay in my lane, maintain, never complain"
"Flow so cold, it's frozen, chosen, spoken golden"
"Money stacking, never slacking, always attacking"
"On the grind, peace of mind, one of a kind"

## Multisyllabic Rhymes
Complex multi-syllable rhyme patterns

"Astronomical, chronological, phenomenal"
"Unpredictable, incomprehensible, unforgettable"
"Revolutionary, extraordinary, legendary"
"Dedicated, elevated, celebrated, validated"

## Common End Rhyme Words (Grouped by Sound)

### -AY Family
day, way, say, play, stay, pay, slay, pray, grey, hey, they, away, okay, delay, replay, display, betray, decay

### -OW Family
flow, go, show, know, grow, low, slow, blow, throw, glow, snow, pro, no, so, dough, though

### -INE Family
line, mine, shine, fine, divine, grind, time, rhyme, climb, prime, sublime, design, define, combine

### -IGHT Family
night, fight, right, light, sight, might, tight, write, bite, flight, height, bright, ignite, delight

### -ACK Family
back, track, stack, black, crack, attack, pack, lack, fact, exact, impact, attract

### -EAT/EET Family
beat, street, heat, meet, feat, seat, treat, repeat, compete, defeat, complete, elite, sweet, greet

### -AIN Family
rain, pain, chain, brain, train, main, gain, strain, maintain, explain, contain, remain, obtain, domain
